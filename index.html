CataShellyst

SUSTAINABLE BIOCATALYST PRODUCTION FROM HAZELNUT SHELLS FOR ENHANCED BIOFUEL EFFICIENCY

About the Project

CataShellyst, cutting-edge process combines enzyme technology with biochar derived from sustainable hazelnut shell waste.


Challenges and Solutions

Traditional biofuel production methods are not preferred due to their low efficiency compared to fossil fuels usage. The catalyst that we produce provides solutions to these problems.

Mission

To provide innovative biocatalyst solutions with the goal of zero emissions, supporting the sustainable biofuel sector, minimizing environmental impacts, and maximizing energy efficiency.

Vision

To offer innovations in biocatalyst technologies and build a sustainable, zero-emission future in the biofuel sector.

Core Values

  • Sustainability
  • Innovation and Technology
  • Energy Efficiency
  • Social Responsibility
